About Jamaican Economics

This page contains the latest international trade data for Jamaica, including service trade data, and tariffs. In 2018 Jamaica was the number 121 economy in the world in terms of GDP (current US$), the number 143 in total exports, the number 135 in total imports, and the number 79 most complex economy according to the Economic Complexity Index (ECI). In 2018, Jamaica exported $1.25B and imported $4.4B, resulting in a negative trade balance of -$3.15B. In 2018, Jamaica's exports per capita were $425 and its imports per capita were $1.5k.
